<p>13% of CISSP exam portion will be from Security Operation domain which covers a vast range of security topics and has further ten sub objectives.</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list"><li>Ability to perform security investigations</li><li>Able to gather evidence, perform digital forensics etc. Knowledge of law and regulations among operational, criminal and civil and the role of digital evidence in a legal discovery process</li><li>Able to log and monitor digital activities, how to design, install and implement intrusion detection and preventions systems, event management, network monitoring etc.</li><li>Provisioning of secure computing resources including hardware/software inventory, implement a configuration management system, securing cloud and virtual resources.</li><li>In depth knowledge of Service Level Agreements (SLAs), Least Privilege, Separation of duties, shift rotations and the information life cycle is covered in fifth objective.</li><li>Able to employ resource protection techniques, able to utilize protection techniques for software/hardware assets and media management.</li><li>Management of incident detection, response, mitigation, reporting, recovery.</li><li>Operating and maintaining preventative security controls like firewalls, IPS, IDS and third-party security services etc.</li><li>Design and implement patch management systems, configuration management systems etc.</li><li>Protecting information and assets and making them available 24/7, implementation of disaster recovery plans, business and continuity processes, physical and personnel security, etc.</li></ol>
